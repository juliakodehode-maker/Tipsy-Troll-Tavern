WHO WE ARE

"Before crowns ruled the land and roads were safe to walk, a lone fire burned beside the old tradeway. From that fire rose the Golden Tankard — a hall sworn to shelter the brave and the doomed alike. Blades were bound by oath within its walls, and no blood was spilled beneath its beams. Kings drank beside cutthroats, and many who planned great deeds here were never seen again. Their stories, however, linger still. The ale is brewed as it was in the first age — strong, golden, and unforgiving. To drink deeply is to share in the courage of those who came before. The Tankard endures. Its hearth yet burns. And every tale demands a price."

"Drink well. Speak true. Leave a story behind."
